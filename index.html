Alice's BlogPost About Sanrio

Pom Pom Purin is a cartoon golden retriever character. Pom Pom Purin is called "Purin" (japanese for pudding) because it looks like a pudding! It's favorite drink is milk and cream caramel pudding!

Kerropi is a cartoon frog who wears a white and blue striped shirt. The name "Kerropi" means a "giant leaps bring faith and forgiveness to all." His favorite thing to do is to hang out with his friends.

Hello Kitty is one of the most popular japanese cartoon characters and is recognized internationally. Her original design and red bow make her recognizable. Her favorite activites are playing piano and baking!
